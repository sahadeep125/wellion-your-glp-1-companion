Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Our Commitment

Wellion is built with a privacy-first architecture. Your health data — including weight, dose logs, check-ins, and any biometric information — is processed on your device. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties for advertising or data brokering purposes.

2. Data We Collect

On-device data: Dose logs, weight entries, check-in responses, and insights are stored locally on your device. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly enable backup or sync features.

Account data (optional): If you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication and subscription management purposes.

Analytics: We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage analytics (e.g., feature usage frequency) to improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide and improve the Wellion app experience
  • To process your subscription through App Store or Google Play
  • To send important service updates (if you have an account)
  • To generate on-device insights, trends, and reports

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal health data with third parties. Subscription payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) — we do not store your payment information.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your on-device data remains on your device. You can delete all data at any time from the app settings. If you have an account, you can request complete account deletion by contacting our support team.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data that is transmitted or stored. On-device data benefits from your device's built-in security features including encryption.

7. Children's Privacy

Wellion is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email if you have an account.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our Support page.
