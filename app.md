# SEO Keyword Research

## Strategy

The high-volume heads ("ozempic", "wegovy") are owned by medical publishers (Mayo, Healthline, Drugs.com) and are informational, not download intent. The winnable, converting space is:

1. **App-intent keywords** — someone actively looking for a tracker app → highest conversion.
2. **Task/question long-tails** — someone with a problem the app solves (missed dose, plateau, side effects, injection rotation) → captured via guide pages, converted with "here's how to do it in Wellion".
3. **Privacy angle** — differentiator no competitor owns: "GLP-1 tracker without account / private".
