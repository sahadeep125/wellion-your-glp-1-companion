Missed an Ozempic Dose? Here's the 5-Day Rule (and How to Never Miss Again)

It's Thursday and you suddenly realize your Ozempic day was Tuesday. Don't panic — there's a clear manufacturer rule for this, and it depends on one thing: how many days have passed.

The 5-day rule for a missed Ozempic dose

Novo Nordisk's official guidance for a missed weekly semaglutide dose (Ozempic and Wegovy) is:

Within 5 days of the missed dose: inject as soon as you remember, then return to your regular schedule.

More than 5 days late: skip the missed dose entirely and take your next dose on the regularly scheduled day. Never take two doses to catch up.

Why 5 days? Semaglutide has a half-life of about a week, so within that window your medication level hasn't dropped far and a late dose won't stack dangerously with the next one. Past it, injecting late would push the two doses too close together and raise the risk of nausea and other side effects.

Mounjaro and Zepbound (tirzepatide) use a slightly different window — Eli Lilly's guidance is 4 days (96 hours). Same principle: late but within the window, take it; beyond it, skip.

What to expect after a missed dose

  • Within the window: most people notice nothing. Take the dose, log it, move on.
  • Skipping a full week: you may notice appetite returning and, if you take it for diabetes, higher glucose readings. That's expected and recovers once you resume.
  • Multiple missed weeks: talk to your prescriber before restarting — after a long gap, they may restart you at a lower dose to avoid a wave of side effects.

One more thing: if you take your late dose on a different weekday, your schedule may shift. Note your new dose day — this is exactly the bookkeeping that trackers exist for.

Frequently asked questions

Can I take two Ozempic doses close together to catch up?

No. Never take an extra or double dose to make up for a missed one. If more than 5 days have passed, skip the missed dose and resume your normal schedule. Doubling up significantly raises the risk of severe nausea and hypoglycemia.

Will I gain weight if I miss one Ozempic dose?

One missed dose within the 5-day window has minimal effect because semaglutide stays in your system for weeks. You might notice slightly more hunger. Missing several doses in a row is when appetite and weight typically rebound.

Can I change my Ozempic injection day permanently?

Yes — you can change your dose day as long as at least 2 days (48 hours) pass between doses. Confirm with your prescriber, then update your dose day in Wellion so reminders follow the new schedule.
