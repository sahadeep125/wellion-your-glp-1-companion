Tirzepatide Guide

Zepbound & Mounjaro Dose Tracking: The Tirzepatide Schedule Explained

Tirzepatide — sold as Zepbound for weight management and Mounjaro for type 2 diabetes — is a dual GIP/GLP-1 weekly injection with six dose levels. More levels means more titration decisions, and more reason to keep a clean record.

The tirzepatide dose ladder

WeeksWeekly doseNotes
1–42.5 mgStarter dose — for adaptation, not therapeutic effect
5–85 mgFirst maintenance-eligible dose
9–127.5 mgIncrease in 2.5 mg steps, each held ≥ 4 weeks
13–1610 mgCommon maintenance dose
17–2012.5 mgIf further escalation is needed and tolerated
21+15 mgMaximum dose

Unlike Wegovy's fixed five steps, tirzepatide maintenance can be 5, 10, or 15 mg (Zepbound) — your prescriber picks the landing spot based on your results and tolerability. That makes your personal response record the key input to the decision.

Tirzepatide's missed dose rule: 4 days, not 5

If you miss a Zepbound or Mounjaro dose, Eli Lilly's guidance is to take it within 4 days (96 hours); after that, skip it and resume your normal schedule. Never double up. (Semaglutide's window is 5 days — if you've switched medications, don't carry the old rule over.)

What to track on tirzepatide

  • Dose and date of every injection — six possible strengths makes "what was I on last month?" a real question.
  • Injection site — same rotation rules as other GLP-1s: abdomen, thigh, upper arm, rotated weekly.
  • Side effects per step — nausea and digestive symptoms typically spike for 1–2 weeks after each increase, then fade. A log shows whether yours are fading or persisting.
  • Weight trend, not daily weight — tirzepatide's trial results are strong, but week-to-week loss is uneven. Trend lines beat single weigh-ins.

Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between Zepbound and Mounjaro?

Both are tirzepatide made by Eli Lilly. Mounjaro is approved for type 2 diabetes; Zepbound is approved for chronic weight management and obstructive sleep apnea. The molecule, pen, and dose ladder are the same.

What is the maintenance dose of Zepbound?

Zepbound maintenance is 5 mg, 10 mg, or 15 mg weekly depending on your response and tolerability — your prescriber decides after the escalation phase. Tracking your weight and side effects at each step informs that choice.
