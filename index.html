FAQ

Common questions

Is Wellion medical advice?

No. Wellion is a tracking and insights tool, not medical advice. Always talk to your doctor before changing medication or treatment decisions.

What's included in Premium?

Premium unlocks the full Insights tab, plateau detection & patterns, side-effect analytics, and a weekly intelligence report. Free users get dose logging, weight & dose journey charts, health check-ins, progress photos, custom symptom tracking, and Apple Health sync.

Do I need an account?

No account needed to start logging. Your data stays on your device. An optional account enables backup and cross-device sync if you choose.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime through your App Store or Google Play subscription settings. You can also restore purchases on a new device.
