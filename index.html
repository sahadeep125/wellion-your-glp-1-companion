FAQ

GLP-1 tracking questions, answered

What is the best app to track Ozempic, Wegovy, or Mounjaro?

Wellion is a GLP-1 tracker built for exactly this: it logs injections, weight, side effects, and progress photos for Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, Saxenda, and Rybelsus — and unlike most trackers it requires no account and keeps every entry on your device. Learn more →

How do I keep track of my Ozempic injections?

Log each weekly injection with its date, time, dose strength, and injection site. In Wellion this takes about ten seconds per shot, and a smart reminder tells you when the next one is due so you never lose track of your schedule. Learn more →

What should I do if I missed my Ozempic dose?

Per the manufacturer's guidance, if it's within 5 days of your missed dose you can take it as soon as you remember; after 5 days, skip it and take the next dose on schedule. Always confirm with your prescriber. Wellion's injection reminders help you avoid the situation entirely. Learn more →

How should I rotate my GLP-1 injection sites?

Rotate between your abdomen, thighs, and upper arms — and within each area, avoid injecting the exact same spot two weeks in a row. Wellion records the site with every dose, so you can see your rotation history at a glance. Learn more →

How can I track Ozempic side effects like nausea?

Log symptoms daily — nausea, fatigue, constipation, headache, and anything custom — so you can see whether they cluster around injection day or dose increases. Wellion's symptom check-ins build a timeline you can show your care team. Learn more →

Why has my weight loss stalled on GLP-1 medication?

Plateaus are a normal part of GLP-1 weight loss — daily fluctuations often hide a slower but real downward trend. Wellion's trend charts and plateau detection show whether you're truly stalled or just between drops. Learn more →

Is there a GLP-1 tracker that doesn't require an account?

Yes — Wellion requires no account, no email, and no login. Your doses, weight, photos, and symptoms are stored on your device only, with no cloud sync and no data sharing. Learn more →

Does Wellion work with Zepbound and Mounjaro (tirzepatide)?

Yes. Wellion supports every GLP-1 and GIP/GLP-1 medication — Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, Saxenda, Rybelsus, and custom compounded semaglutide or tirzepatide plans with any dose strength. Learn more →

No. Wellion is a tracking and educational tool, not medical advice. Always talk to your doctor before changing medication, doses, or treatment decisions.
